For Stats - Actuarial Science Major


       

ACCT:250 Spreadsheet Modeling and Decision Analysis
(must to have completed 24cr to enroll)
This is Intro-to-Excel class from Business School. It is not officially listed as Stats core requirement, but it is a very good idea for all Stats major to take this class. Offered every semester.

CPSC: 200 Programming for Data Science (4cr)
MW 2:00 - 3:40
One of Stats Core requirement. All Stats majors have to take either CPSC209 or CPSC200. CPSC200 uses Python, and it is better one to take for Stat-AS major. All Stat-AS majors who haven't taken CPSC209 or CPSC200 really should take this course when it's offered. Offered randomly.

STAT 480 Statistical Data Management
TR 3:30 - 4:45 Ye
Pre-requisite: STAT 461 Applied Stats.
One of Stat Core requirement. After Applied Stats, you should take this class before any other Stats course. So take it if you haven't. Offered every Fall.

STAT 451 Theo Stat I
TR 5:15-6:30 Datta
Pre-requisite: STAT 461 Applied Stats. One of Stats Core requirement. You need Calc II completed, and taking Calc III to register for this class. Offered every Fall.

STAT 495 - Statistical Consulting
TR 2 - 2:50 Ye
If you are a senior (2 semesters left to graduate), you should enroll. Enroll for 2 credits in the second last semester of your study, and enroll for 1 credit in your last semester. Offered every semester.

STAT:484 Introduction to Machine Learning
W 3:30 - 4:45 (50% online) Mimoto
Pre-requisite: STAT 461 Applied Stats.
One of Stat-AS Core requirement. It is better if you take Reg & ANOVA, and Stat Data Mgmt before this class. Offered every Fall.

ECON:244 Intro to Economic Analysis
One of Stat-AS requirements. Alternatively, Stat-AS student can take both of ECON:200 Prin of Micro & ECON:201 Prin of Macro instead of ECON:244.

ACCT:201 Accounting Principles I
Many section offered. One of Stat-AS requirements. Grade of B- or better fulfills Society of Actuary VEE requirement. Offered every semester.

FIN:301 Principles of Finance
Many section offered. One of Stat-AS requirements. Grade of B- or better fulfills Society of Actuary VEE requirement. Offered every semester.

FIN:343 Investments
RMI 414 - Risk Managment: Property and Casualty
RMI 415 - Risk Management: Life and Health Insurance
Stat-AS majors must take one of above 3.

STAT:486 Spatial-Temporal Statistics
TR 12:15-1:30 Ye Pre-requisite: STAT 461 Applied Stats. One of Stat Electives. All Stat-AS majors have to have 1 Stats electives to graduate. Usually offered once every 2 years.
